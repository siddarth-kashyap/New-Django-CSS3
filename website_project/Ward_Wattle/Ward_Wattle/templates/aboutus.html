This is a website to represent any Company or its Business to reach its clients & audiences out-there.
This website is developed on Django Framework and designed by scratch on CSS3. There is no help taken from bootstrap. It is purely coded on CSS3 line by line.
It has responsive layout for three main resolutions which are full- large display (1200px or more), than medium resolution (768px or more) and last for small resoultion (320px or less).
It comes with carousel effect on the Home Page showing the services provided by Company. It has Contact Us module in which a client can contact Company with their query or message and share his/her personal details including E-mail ID.
